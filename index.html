what is LIVESHADES

At liveshades we believe every event should be unique and connected to the people in attendance. We bring together the most inspiring local artists, create special experiences for our guests, and make sure everything goes as planned. And we are always on the lookout for ways to improve and feel alive and get a sense of the vibe!
LIVESHADES is a team of creative and inspiring people who are passionate about producing events that connect people, bring new ideas to life and fuel your passion for the arts. We believe in the power of creativity, collaboration and purpose to change lives.
